Reasons why you should learn Programming

You Can Create Anything You Want

Computer programming, like no other job, gives you the opportunity to digitize your ideas. creating an app, it’s not an easy task as it requires effort, time, and money. let’s be honest, no one can implement your idea exactly how you want it to be.

You Understand How Software Works

Programming will get you familiar with the software, programs, devices, and websites and will help you understand them. Due to this familiarization, you will grasp and adopt new technology tools much more quickly.

To improve your problem-solving and abstraction skills

Learning programming is more or less coding and determining how to tackle the issues that arise during coding and finding solutions for them. The more you get to solve these problems, the more practical it becomes.

To satisfy your curiosity

We are surrounded by all types of devices: desktop computers, laptops, tablets, phones… and we are all the time using their programs and applications. Would you like to get a better understanding of the devices you are working with every day? The time is now

Why Learn JavaScript?

Compared to many other programming languages like JAVA, PYTHON, or PHP, JavaScript offers one of the more beginner-friendly entry points into the world of programming.

Is PHP Worth Learning In 2021?

PHP is still one of the most popular programming languages on the web. Sites such as Facebook.com, Microsoft.com, wikipedia.org, realtor.com, salesforce.com, slack.com, tesla.com, etsy.com, and of course, wordpress.com are all running on PHP. Over 70% of the web uses PHP as their server-side programming language.
